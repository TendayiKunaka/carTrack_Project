About City Cars

Our Mission

City Cars aims to simplify vehicle management for residents, police officers, and parking attendants through a unified platform. We provide transparency in vehicle history, streamline ticket management, and offer financial flexibility when needed.

How It Works

Our system integrates with city databases to provide accurate vehicle information. Users can register their vehicles, search for any vehicle's history, manage tickets, and access small loans for vehicle-related expenses. Police and parking attendants can issue tickets with photo evidence directly through the system.
